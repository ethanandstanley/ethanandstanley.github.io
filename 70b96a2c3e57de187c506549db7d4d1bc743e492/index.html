About You

Being a surrogate is a tremendous act of generosity. The wonderwomen who offer to carry a pregnancy for someone else are incredibly loving, strong, and altruistic humans. We know that, most of all, our surrogate will be someone who believes in her heart that she wants to help us – two gay guys – have a child. With our surrogacy agency, we will do everything we can to support our surrogate through the process, providing emotional guidance with a social worker, legal support, and financial compensation.

Based on our clinic’s screening process a good candidate needs to meet a few requirements:

  • 21-40 years old
  • US Citizen living in a surrogacy friendly state (Most states are friendly! The exceptions are: MI, NE, LA, IN, AZ)
  • Had one successful pregnancy and delivered at least one child
  • No more than 3 cesarian sections
  • BMI of less than 33
  • Non-smoker, Non-drug user
